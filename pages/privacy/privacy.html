Privacy policy

Pepper is a food company. Our platform is intended to provide a seamless experience for creating, sharing, and discovering food from around the world and interacting with the chefs behind that food.

When you use Pepper, you’ll share information with us so we can enhance your personal and community-wide experience on the platform. We want to be upfront about the information we collect, how we use it, where we use it, whom we share it with, and how you can customize your privacy choices to ensure your maximum comfort with the extent of the information you share.

That leads us to our Privacy Policy. We’ve composed our policy in a way that’s refreshingly free of the complicated legal jargon that overwhelms typical privacy policies. We want you to spend more time cooking and buttering up your friend’s meals and less time searching for how your information is being used.

So we’ve done our best to make our policy clear, concise, and respectful of your privacy. Of course, if you still have any questions about anything in our Privacy Policy, just contact us.

Information

We collect a wide range of information from our users to maintain, enhance, and personalize our platform and services. Below is a more detailed picture of what type of information we use.

Information You Provide

When you use Pepper, we collect information that you provide to us. For example, when you first set up a Pepper account, we collect a few important details about you (your name, username, password, email address, phone number, date of birth, location). We also give you the option to provide additional information that will be publicly visible on Pepper, such as a profile picture and a short bio.

You will also provide us with the information you share on Pepper, such as Recipes and Messages. Remember, the internet isn’t written in pencil, it’s written in ink. We do not prohibit users from screenshotting or saving in any form the Recipes and Chats shared with them on the platform. So please don’t share content that you wouldn’t want everyone in the world to see.

We also collect the information you provide when you contact user support or communicate with us in any other form and any information we need to resolve your inquiry.

Information We Get When You Use Pepper

When you use Pepper, we collect information on how you use Pepper. For example, we gather data on actions such as the time you spend on different pages, if you saw a specific Recipe or ad, your frequency of logins, and more

Here’s a better explanation of the types of information we collect when you use Pepper...

  • Usage: We collect information on your activity on Pepper. In more detail, we collect information on:
    • How you interact with Pepper, such as your search queries, which Recipes you like, what kinds of Recipes you spend the most time on in Discover, whether you’re joining Kitchens, among other interactions
    • How you communicate with other Chefs, such as their names, how often you interact with their posts (liking, commenting, uploading your own version, etc.), the time and date of your messages, the number of messages you exchange, which friends you interact and message the most, and how often you interact with Chefs outside your following
  • Content: We collect the content you create and share on Pepper, such as custom Recipes, and the information on the content you create, such as the interactions with the content (likes, comments, impressions)
  • Device: We collect information from and about the devices you use to access Pepper. For example, we collect:
    • Information about the hardware and software on your device, including but not limited to hardware model, OS version, memory, advertising identifiers, unique application identifiers, apps installed, unique device identifiers, browser type, language, battery level, and time zone
    • Information from device sensors (accelerometers, gyroscopes, compasses, microphones, and connected audio devices
    • Information about your wireless and mobile network connections, including but not limited to mobile phone number, service provider, IP address and signal strength
  • Device Phonebook. Pepper is all about socializing and sharing food with friends, so we ask to collect information from your device’s phonebook to better connect you with your social circles!
  • Camera and Photos. We request permission to access to your device’s camera and photos in order to collect images and allow you to access your pictures to upload to Pepper
  • Location.We request permission to access to collect information about your location using technology such as GPS, wireless networks, cell towers, Wi-Fi access points and other sensors from your device information
  • Cookies and Other Technology. We may use cookies and other technology (including but not limited to web beacons, web stores, and unique advertising identifiers) to collect information about your activity, browser, and device. We may also use this information to learn how you interact with our partners or third parties. For example, we can use information on websites you’ve visited to cater more personalized advertisements. You have the option to remove or reject cookies through the settings option in your browser or device.
  • Log. We collect log information when you use our website, including but not limited to access times, pages viewed, device information (web browser, language), information about how you’ve used our services, IP address, cookies or other technology, identity of your device or browser, and pages visited before, during, or after visiting our website

How We Use Information

Simply put, we use all this information we gather to provide you the best, most personalized and seamless experience using Pepper that we will continue to unforgivingly improve every chance and new idea we get. Here are some ways we accomplish just that:

  • Improve, maintain, operate, develop further, and protect all things Pepper

How Long We Keep Your Information

Pepper allows you to collect and share all things food. No more messy cookbooks or lost photos of your favorite meals, our platform allows you to store your favorite food in a place you’ll never lose it. We store all your recipes and information for as long as you provide it to us.

For a more detailed timeline on how long we store your information....

  • Basic account information. We store your name, location, phone number, email address, contacts, etc. until you ask us to delete them.
  • Location information. We store location information for different lengths of time. For example, we store locations of a restaurant you posted from for as long as we store the post. We store your current location for as long as you want to share it with us so we can better cater ads, Kitchens, Competitions, and other services we provide.
  • Interactions. We constantly update your information based on your likes and uploads so we can better cater relevant ads to your interests

If you ever get tired of seeing good food and meeting good people and decide to stop using Pepper, you can simply ask us to delete your account. Automatic deletion is designed to be instantaneous, however, some information might take longer due to legal requirements on storing your data, as well as the potential to preserve content for valid legal processes or reports of abuse and other Terms of Service Violations. We may also retain certain information for a limited period of time or as required by law.
